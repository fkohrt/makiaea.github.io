  • the sword of serenity
    • or, the battle
    • how or what to we need to act? The sword/a gateway to action
    • The principle of understanding and working with the flow of natural processes helps us make good use of our resources. It is like when your hand is caught by a cat's claws when you play together — if you know to move towards the cat's paws, rather than away. Moving towards means that the claws can no longer grip you, since they are pointed towards the cat, while moving away helps strengthen the grip and may even help to cause damage if moving away sufficiently quickly. Similar types of principles are espoused in aikido and zen meditation. We will be approaching some everyday processes from this point of view in part 3 of the book, "the sword".
