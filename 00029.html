collaborative card game creator app

  • people can modify and create game mechanics and card designs and share them with each other as whole games or as components that you can add or replace parts of individual games — like a super remix version of anyone's game
  • people could recreate popular games and share them (they wouldn't be allowed on the central server but you might not be able to stop them) and up vote them, make them viral in other ways
  • for example could be used for a kids programming course at school, with arty kids making graphics, programming kids doing game logic, but each and every person could contribute their own piece to the whole.
  • or a freshman class could make a new game that suits their course or role or club or society.
  • or for child's special party or function you could create a special game to share with everyone, or even commission one from others
  • or an event or location can create a game to give away to visitors or potential visitors and even run their own “best contributions from our users” blogs or competitions
  • you could add sounds as well, so could be used for flashcard study, could make up cards based on what you are studying in class and share them with others in games at days end, create a rotation system based on what cards you have learned and rotate them out, have automatic suggestions based on (spaced recognition systems) that suggest cards to rotate back into play
