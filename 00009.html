if it is true that we go in flow when the challenge closely matches our abilities, then:

    1. if your abilities are very different from what is seen to be the norm, and
    1. the challenges designed into the system are inflexibly linked to the norm,
  • then for you to achieve flow you must achieve one or more or similar of the following:
    1. create your own internal challenges,
    1. change the system challenges to make them more suitable or flexible,
    1. change the norm.
